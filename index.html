About

BTS, or Bangtan Sonyeondan, is a South Korean septet that has taken the world by storm with its dynamic blend of musical styles and profound lyricism. Formed in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment, the group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Known for their genre-defying music that spans pop, hip-hop, R&B, and beyond, BTS has become a global cultural phenomenon. Their empowering and introspective lyrics touch on themes such as self-love, mental health, and societal issues, resonating with a diverse and dedicated fanbase known as the "ARMY." Beyond their chart-topping albums like "Love Yourself: Tear" and "Map of the Soul: 7," BTS has garnered accolades worldwide, winning prestigious awards like Billboard Music Awards and American Music Awards. Their electrifying performances and engaging social media presence have solidified their status as not just a musical group but a cultural force. BTS is celebrated not only for their musical achievements but also for their philanthropic endeavors. They've actively supported causes such as UNICEF's "Love Myself" campaign, demonstrating a commitment to making a positive impact on the world. As they continue to break boundaries and captivate audiences globally, BTS stands as a testament to the power of music to connect people across cultures and inspire positive change.

Discography

Take Two

21st century pop icons BTS released “Take Two,” a digital single celebrating the band’s 10th anniversary, on June 9. “Take Two” is an acoustic pop genre song. BTS look back on their decade-long journey since their debut, and picture the forthcoming future together with their fans.

Proof

BTS released the new anthology album Proof on June 10, 11 months after their latest single CD Butter. Proof is the core of BTS’ history. The new three-CD album introduces three new tracks, one in each CD: “Yet To Come (The Most Beautiful Moment)”, “Run BTS” and “For Youth”, each in different genres conveying their own messages.

Butter

BTS released their CD single Butter on July 9. The CD single includes the shimmering summer song “Butter” as well as “Permission to Dance” that will get your heart pumping and dance again. The instrumental versions of each are also part of the tracklist. Belonging to the dance pop genre, “Permission to Dance” sits on piano and string sounds that make the listeners bop their heads and bust some dance moves. World-renowned musician Ed Sheeran and British producer Steve Mac took part in making the song.

BE

"BTS returns with 'BE,' a poignant reflection on 2020. The album encapsulates the collective emotions of fear, anxiety, and determination amid a changing world. Featuring tracks like 'Dynamite' and the lead single 'Life Goes On,' BTS delivers a comforting message that, despite unexpected challenges, life continues, and they extend a reassuring hand towards the future."

DYNAMITE

"BTS's 'Dynamite' is a Disco Pop anthem exuding joy and confidence. As their first English-language release, the song is a global sensation, offering a burst of much-needed energy. In the pursuit of overcoming challenges, BTS embraces a new, uplifting chapter, showcasing their distinctive charms through infectious choreography."

MAP OF THE SOUL: 7

"BTS drops 4th album 'MAP OF THE SOUL : 7,' a heartfelt journey of self-discovery. Reflecting on seven years since debut, the album acknowledges both the joys and struggles, embracing the scars that shaped their true selves. Through art and music, BTS shares their growth, connecting with fans on a deeper level."

LOVE YOURSELF

"BTS concludes their LOVE YOURSELF series with 'ANSWER,' a celebration with ARMY. The narrative, initiated in 2016, emphasizes the message of self-love. 'ANSWER,' the final piece, includes seven new tracks, showcasing BTS' lyrical prowess and storytelling as artists."

YOU NEVER WALK ALONE

"BTS releases 'YOU NEVER WALK ALONE,' the epilogue to WINGS, bringing closure to the narrative of youth and growth. The album delivers a message of comfort and hope, emphasizing the power of togetherness for the youth of this generation."

WINGS

"BTS is set to release their second studio album, 'WINGS,' on October 10. The album explores themes of boys facing temptation, experiencing pain, and seeking bliss, portrayed metaphorically as birds breaking out of their shells and attempting to take flight for the first time."

YOUNG FOREVER

"BTS concludes 'The Most Beautiful Moment in Life' series with the release of the special album 'Young Forever' on May 2. This marks the final chapter of their epic journey, encapsulating the stories of young people navigating an uncertain reality yet continuing to surge forward with resilience and determination."

DARK & WILD

"BTS returns with 'DARK & WILD,' their latest studio album after a year. The tracks explore love complexities, showcasing a matured image of BTS. 'Danger,' the title track, is a powerful hybrid hip-hop song, highlighting the group's evolved musical direction with impactful rap and exhilarating elements."

O!RUL8,2?

"BTS's album 'O!RUL8,2?' delivers a powerful message: Find your happiness and life before it's too late. The lyrics brutally expose the harsh reality of a world pressuring children to be studying machines, devoid of future contemplation. The album reflects on the wounds and anguish inflicted by a competitive and despairing environment on today's teens."

Member's

KIM NAMJOON

Kim Namjoon, known as RM, is the leader and rapper of BTS. Renowned for his exceptional lyricism and thoughtful insights, RM plays a crucial role in shaping BTS's identity. His leadership and artistic contributions have been instrumental in the group's global success.

KIM SEOKJIN

Kim Seokjin, often referred to as Jin, is the eldest member of BTS and holds the position of vocalist and visual. With his charming personality and powerful vocals, Jin contributes significantly to BTS's musical and visual appeal. He brings a combination of talent and charisma to the group.

MIN YOON-GI

Min Yoongi, known by his stage name Suga, is BTS's rapper and songwriter. Renowned for his exceptional rap skills and introspective lyrics, Suga is a key creative force in the group. His impact extends beyond music, making him a multifaceted artist and producer.

JUNG HOSEOK

Jung Hoseok, also known as J-Hope, is BTS's versatile dancer, rapper, and songwriter. His vibrant stage presence, exceptional dance moves, and positive energy make him a standout performer. J-Hope contributes significantly to BTS's dynamic and diverse musical style.

PARK JIMIN

Park Jimin, commonly known as Jimin, is BTS's charismatic and talented vocalist and dancer. With his soulful voice and captivating stage presence, Jimin adds depth to the group's performances. Known for his impressive dance skills, he is a key contributor to BTS's success.

KIM TAEHYUNG

Kim Taehyung, often known as V, is BTS's multi-talented artist renowned for his deep, velvety vocals and unique stage presence. As a singer, songwriter, and actor, V's artistic versatility shines, captivating fans worldwide. His distinctive voice and charming personality make him a standout member of BTS.

JEON JEONG-GUK

Jeon Jungkook, the youngest member of BTS, is a prodigious talent in singing, dancing, and songwriting. Known for his powerful vocals and dynamic performances, Jungkook has become a global sensation. His charisma and dedication to his craft make him a vital part of BTS's success.
